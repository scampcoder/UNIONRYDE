RYDE HARD OR GO HOME

There's really only one "rule" at UnionRYDE: Ryde hard! But there are a few policies in place to make sure things go smoothly for everyone. #unifyed

READY. STEADY. RYDE.

You should expect to get your sweat on and have fun! All of our UnionRYDE classes are high energy 45 minutes and we push your limits every class. Each class starts with a warm-up and ends with a cool down and stretching on the bike.
Please be sure to hydrate throughout the day and have a light snack at least a half hour prior to starting your RYDE.
We love newbies! Please arrive to class at least 15 minutes early to fill out a waiver and receive instruction that will help you get the most out of your RYDE. ICYMI, we have an awesome New RYDER Special: create an account with us and we'll give you 1 FREE RYDE.
All fitness levels are welcome. UnionRYDE classes are designed to challenge you and are led with a “we do this together” attitude. We encourage you to listen to your body and go at your own pace if needed.
We recommend athletic gear that is breathable but nothing overly baggy. For safety reasons, we require UnionRYDERS to clip in to the pedals. Rentals are available for $2 per RYDE with the first two free. All bikes are compatible with LOOK Delta and SPD cleats.
All you need are socks, a water bottle and a smile! We take care of the rest. We provide an alkaline water filling station, and we sell water bottles if you forget yours. We also provide workout towels for use during class and chilled eucalyptus towels during cool down.
Each of our UnionCREW members has their own unique style of RYDE and their personality comes through in their choice of music and instruction. We encourage you to try out classes with a variety of our UnionCREW to find the class that works best for YOU.

BOOKING YOUR RYDE

The schedule is on a revolving seven-day calendar, so it’s always available for you to book your classes online when it’s convenient for you. Classes for the following week are posted at 4 pm Sunday. Spots go fast so book your bike early if you have a favorite spot to get your RYDE on.
Classes fill up fast, especially for popular times and instructors. We offer a waitlist for your convenience. Chances are good that you will get a spot in class. If the class you want is full, add yourself to the waitlist. You will get an email no later than the night before class notifying you if you have been added to class. Contact the studio if you’re wondering where you are on the list to help gauge your chances. If you’re feeling lucky, you can take a chance and show up at class time to get a spot in case someone doesn’t show up.
We ask that you cancel 12 hours before your class to avoid a cancellation fee of $10.The 12 hour window allows us to offer a bike to another RYDER. We know sometimes things come up, and you may need to late cancel. If you cancel after the 12 hour window before your RYDE, there is a fee of $10 NO EXCEPTIONS. If you don’t have a card on file for the cancellation policy, you will forfeit that class. If you book a spot and don’t make it to class (without notifying the studio or cancelling in your account) there is a $10 no-show fee.
To cancel your reservation, log in to your account under My Classes and click Unreserve. You can also call the studio at 714.389.0135 (Tustin) or email us. CANCELLATIONS CANNOT BE MADE VIA FACEBOOK OR TEXT MESSAGE.
Please call the studio at 714.389.0135 (Tustin) and let us know. If you're not checked in at least 2 minutes before class, another RYDER from our waitlist could claim your seat. For your safety, and that of other RYDERS we cannot allow you to enter the spin room after the first song. You will forfeit your RYDE.
