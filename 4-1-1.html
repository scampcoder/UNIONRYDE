RYDE HARD OR GO HOME

There's really only one "rule" at UnionRYDE: Ryde hard! But there are a few policies in place to make sure things go smoothly for everyone. #unifyed

READY. STEADY. RYDE.

You should expect to get your sweat on and have fun! All of our UnionRYDE classes are high energy 45 minutes and we push your limits every class. Each class starts with a warm-up and ends with a cool down and stretching on the bike.
Please be sure to hydrate throughout the day and have a light snack at least a half hour prior to starting your RYDE.
We love newbies! Please arrive to class at least 15 minutes early to fill out a waiver and receive instruction that will help you get the most out of your RYDE. ICYMI, we have an awesome New RYDER Special: create an account with us and we'll give you 1 FREE RYDE.
All fitness levels are welcome. UnionRYDE classes are designed to challenge you and are led with a “we do this together” attitude. We encourage you to listen to your body and go at your own pace if needed.
We recommend athletic gear that is breathable but nothing overly baggy. For safety reasons, we require UnionRYDERS to clip in to the pedals. Rentals are available for $2 per RYDE with the first two free. All bikes are compatible with LOOK Delta and SPD cleats.
All you need are socks, a water bottle and a smile! We take care of the rest. We provide an alkaline water filling station, and we sell water bottles if you forget yours. We also provide workout towels for use during class and chilled eucalyptus towels during cool down.
Each of our UnionCREW members has their own unique style of RYDE and their personality comes through in their choice of music and instruction. We encourage you to try out classes with a variety of our UnionCREW to find the class that works best for YOU.
